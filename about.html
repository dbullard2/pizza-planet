About Us

Serving pizza that's out of this world since 1995

Founded by Aldo McCarthy in 1995, Pizza Planet has been serving the best pizza in Oakland ever since. Pies, slices, wings, salads, deserts, and more. All available for dine-in, take-out, or delivery. We pride ourselves in offering hand-tossed pizzas made with the freshest ingredients including daily, made-from-scratch dough and sauce. Our reputation has been built on serving the highest quality food at reasonable prices.
